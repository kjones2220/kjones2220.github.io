But First, A Little About Me

I am the 90's lovin', hip-hop playing, pop culture obsessed consumer insights analyst with a side hustle of graphic design. If you ask me Biggie or Tupac, I'll say Biggie in a heartbeat. If you ask Photoshop or Illustrator, expect to hear the one and only correct answer –– Illustrator.

I'm a senior Marketing and Communication double major with a passion for... you probably thought I'd say fashion in reference to Sasha from Bratz. But, my passion is for everything that exudes spunk. I love understanding people, why they do what they do, and how to best appeal to them. So, it makes sense that consumer insights is for me.

I've been in love with the media and entertainment industry for so long that I can't tell if my passion for it started first or if it was my performance as Scary Spice from the Spice Girls in the talent show. With graphic design, you take in all of the details about your target audience –– or maybe just your friend who wants a snapchat filter for their birthday –– and you bring their vision to life. You listen and then you deliver. That's what it's all about.
